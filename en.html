President: Broker Chien Wu-Hsiang
2025–26 · 49th Charter Anniversary Gala Welcome Address
Yang Ming 49 · Shining Forever

Distinguished Governor, fellow Rotarians, dear members and families — good evening!

Tonight, on behalf of the Rotary Club of Taipei Yang Ming, I extend my most sincere welcome to our sister clubs from abroad — the Kyoto Murasaki-no Club, the Osaka Tenmabashi Club, and the Tokyo Tsuchiura Club from Japan; the Manila Metro Club from the Philippines; and the New Territories Club and Lan Kwai Fong Club from Hong Kong. I also warmly welcome the Yanping Club, Dalongdong Club, and my most esteemed Dadaocheng Club from District 3482, as well as members and families from Division 2 and Division 8 of District 3521. Thank you all for being here to witness the glorious moment of "Yang Ming 49 — Shining Forever".

As we stride toward our golden jubilee of 50 years, the Rotary Club of Taipei Yang Ming has always been dedicated to putting the Rotary spirit into practice. In the 2025–26 Rotary year, we partnered with the Children's Cultural Arts Foundation and, with the strong support of our parent club, the Rotary Club of Taipei North, and the Rotary Club of Taipei Dadaocheng in District 3482, launched the "Tong-Hua Taipei — Children's Hometown Story Picture Book Creation" service project. With the commitment of teachers and students at Yanping, Dalong, Taiping, and Zhongxiao Elementary Schools, the picture book — a year's labor of love — will be officially unveiled on May 2 at the Shu-Shin Hall of Nishi Honganji in Ximending. This work is not only the creative achievement of our children, but also the most precious memory we leave for Taipei. To all the principals, teachers, and students who participated, I offer my highest respect and gratitude.

The warmth and growth of the Yang Ming Club are owed to the support of our past presidents, members, and families. Thank you all for your understanding and encouragement of my wife, Ya-Jing, and me over this past year. It is your participation and dedication that have built this family brimming with solidarity. The Yang Ming family spirit will surely pass the baton from generation to generation, shining forever!

I also have an honor to share with you: in preparation for the 2026 Rotary International Convention in Taipei, the RI President has requested that 108 club presidents be selected from Taiwan's 9 districts to serve as flag bearers at the opening ceremony. I am deeply honored to have been recommended by our District Governor to represent the Yang Ming Club in this important role. This is not just a personal honor — it is the pride of the entire Yang Ming Club.

Finally, my special thanks to PE David, our organizing committee chair, for his hard work and dedication in making this charter anniversary celebration a success. Please enjoy the banquet. Wishing you all good health and peace of mind. See you on June 13 at the Taipei International Convention!

Broker Chien Wu-Hsiang
President 2025–26, Rotary Club of Taipei Yang Ming
