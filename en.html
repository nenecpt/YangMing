2025–26 President Broker Chien Wu-Hsiang
49th Charter Anniversary Gala
Welcome Address
Yang Ming 49 · Shining Forever

Distinguished Governor, fellow Rotarians, dear members and families — good evening!

Tonight marks the 49th birthday of our beloved Yang Ming Club. It is a great joy to be gathered here together to celebrate this special evening.

On behalf of the Rotary Club of Taipei Yang Ming, I extend my warmest welcome to our sister clubs who have traveled from afar — the Kyoto Murasaki-no Club, the Osaka Tenmabashi Club, and the Tokyo Tsuchiura Club from Japan; the Manila Metro Club from the Philippines; and the New Territories Club and Lan Kwai Fong Rotary Club from Hong Kong. Thank you for crossing seas and borders to celebrate with us! I also warmly welcome the clubs of District 3521's Division 2 — Bailing, Fuling, Yang Xing, Hualing, and Yueling; Division 8 — Huagang, Huapeng, Huayou, and Jiuhe; as well as District 3482's Yanping and Dalongdong clubs, and our dearest friends and families from the Dadaocheng Club. With all of you here, tonight's celebration of "Yang Ming 49 — Shining Forever" is truly complete.

As we approach our golden jubilee, the Yang Ming Club has throughout its history upheld the spirit of Rotary, never absent from district affairs, divisional activities, or community service. This year is no exception — in partnership with the Children's Cultural Arts Foundation, and with the support of our parent club, the Rotary Club of Taipei North, and the Rotary Club of Taipei Dadaocheng in District 3482, we launched the "Tong-Hua Taipei — Children's Hometown Story Picture Book Creation" joint community service project. We are grateful to the principals and teachers of Taipei's Yanping, Dalong, Taiping, and Zhongxiao Elementary Schools for their wholehearted support. After nearly a year of creative work, the picture book will be officially unveiled on May 2 at the Shu-Shin Hall of Nishi Honganji in Ximending. Every painting, every story captures Taipei through the pure eyes of its children — the most precious memories one can imagine. To all the principals, teachers, parents, and students who participated, I offer my deepest respect and gratitude!

Having made it through this year successfully, I owe the greatest thanks to the past presidents of our club, every single member, and the wonderful families who have stood by us throughout. Thank you for your understanding, encouragement, and support of Broker and my wife, Ya-Jing. Every event, every piece of club business was made possible only through your dedication. The Yang Ming Club is not just an organization — it is a family, a warm and united family.

I am confident that Yang Ming will continue to pass the baton, generation after generation, shining forever!

I also have some exciting news to share: at the 2026 Rotary International Convention in Taipei, the RI President has expressed the wish for 108 current club presidents from Taiwan's nine districts to serve as flag bearers. I am honored that, with the Governor's recommendation, Broker will represent our Yang Ming Club as one of those 108 flag bearers at the opening ceremony. This honor belongs not to me alone, but to every member of the Yang Ming family!

Finally, my special thanks go to PE David, our gala organizing committee chair, for his dedicated efforts in making tonight's event possible. Now, let us relax and enjoy this wonderful evening together!

Wishing you all good health and peace of mind. See you on June 13 at the Taipei International Convention!

Broker Chien Wu-Hsiang
President 2025–26, Rotary Club of Taipei Yang Ming
